# QKD Systems

## The heart of quantum security

At the center of our infrastructure are the QKD systems themselves. We evaluate and integrate various QKD technologies, including those based on entangled photons and prepare-and-measure approaches. These systems are selected and optimized based on their performance, range, and ability to be integrated into different network environments. Our test fields enable us to operate the QKD systems under real-world conditions and test their robustness against environmental influences and attempted attacks.
